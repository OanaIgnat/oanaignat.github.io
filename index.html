About me

Tenure-Track Assistant Professor at SCU. I'm hiring kind and self-motivated PhD students! More info. PhD Application form!.
PhD and Postdoctoral researcher at UMich, LIT Lab, advised by Rada Mihalcea.
My research is at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision, with current focus on AI for social good and AI for all, towards ensuring inclusive representations in language-vision models and datasets.
For my thesis, I worked with social media videos such as YouTube vlogs, to develop datasets and models for human action understanding.
