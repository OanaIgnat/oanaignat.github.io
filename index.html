About me

Postdoctoral researcher in CS at University of Michigan, LIT Lab, advised by Rada Mihalcea.
I am on the job market for tenure-track Professor positions.
My work lies at the intersection of Natural Language Processing & Computer Vision. Current focus on AI for social good, building demographically & geo-diverse models.
For my thesis, I worked with social media videos such as YouTube vlogs, to develop Datasets & Models for Human Action Understanding.
