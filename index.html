About me

I am a PhD Candidate at University of Michigan, member of the Language and Information Technologies Lab, advised by Rada Mihalcea.
My research is mainly at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision: I work with social media data such as YouTube vlogs and their transcripts.
I am looking to get involved in the AI4Good movement, applying AI in problems that have potential to improve the world (climate change, misinformation, bias, ethics). I am also a fan of hobbies and a healthy work-life balance.
